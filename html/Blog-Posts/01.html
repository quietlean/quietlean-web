#01

There is this kind of electronic music, and I was wondering what it is that makes it feel like there is a common theme. I gave it some thought. At first, I thought it was just something emotional, but that didnt feel quite right. How could I describe it? Everytime I listen to these songs, it feels like something falls right into place. Feels a little bit like that image you have of an ideal human connection, where someone reaches into your head, pulls out the seaweed and gives you a hug. I'm not sure if humans can do that. Some songs feel like I'm taking a hot shower and others make me feel like I've become a forest elf.

So, I figured it'd be a good idea to throw some of these songs into my first playlist. You could argue that a good Playlist needs more contrast, but let's forget about that for today.
